About Echomuir

Echomuir builds precision tools for serious musicians. Not shortcuts. Not subscriptions. Not dark patterns. Just the best way to practice, improve, and know exactly what you're doing.

We stand on:

  • Respect — Transparent, no manipulation, no predatory UX
  • Precision — Accuracy matters; users deserve control
  • Depth without friction — Simple entry point, power available when needed
  • Earned mastery — The tools acknowledge that greatness requires work
