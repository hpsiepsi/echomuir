Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with LochTone?
Open the app and select your bagpipe chanter. LochTone will guide you through the tuning process step by step. Start with a single note and work through each until your chanter is locked in. Detailed settings are available in preferences if you need more control.
Can I use LinneNome with other instruments?
Absolutely. LinneNome works as a metronome for any instrument. Whether you're practicing bagpipes, drums, or any other instrument, the steady rhythm is the same—adjustable tempo, clear beats, nothing else.
What's the best way to use Eastinoto for practice?
Start with a pattern you want to master. Set a comfortable tempo and let it loop for 5–10 minutes. Focus on precision, not speed. As muscle memory builds, gradually increase the tempo. Consistency matters more than duration.
Are there any keyboard shortcuts?
Yes. Most apps support standard shortcuts: Space to start/stop, Arrow keys to adjust tempo, and Escape to reset. Check the app preferences for a full list.
Can I export or save my practice sessions?
Session history is saved automatically in each app. You can review your practice logs in the History section. Export features are coming in future updates.
Do the apps work offline?
Yes. All Echomuir apps are designed to work offline. No internet connection required once installed.
What devices are supported?
Check the app page for specific platform details. All apps are built to work on modern devices without lag or compatibility issues.
